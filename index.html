Experience

Ad-Fraud Research

I'm currently working in a small research project under Professor Zubair Shafiq. The goal of the project is to audit the effectiveness of anti-bot services in detecting ad-fraud bots. I've helped reverse engineer bot detection scripts and run experiments with malicious bot programs.

HackDavis - symptom.space

At HackDavis, I helped to create symptom.space, a web application that streamlines and automates UC Davis's current Daily Symptom Survey process. The application uses a QR code scanning system that validates if a student has completed and passed their symptom survey and tracks their locations at designated buildings for contact tracing. I primarily worked on the front-end and created the QR code scanner that is used to check in students.
